IT Consulting and Outstanding Software

OCTO Technology is an IT consulting company created in France in 1998. Our consultants assist our clients in the digital transformation of their businesses through technology, methodology, but also by deeply understanding their culture and the stakes of their business. From startups to multinationals, OCTO gets involved wherever IT plays a decisive role in transforming companies and their people.

Our culture is made of an optimal balance between technology and interpersonal skills. It’s our culture that made OCTO being voted the Best place Place to work for 5 years in a row in France. OCTO Technology arrived in Australia in 2015 to expand and share this culture and expertise down under. Let us share our culture with you !
